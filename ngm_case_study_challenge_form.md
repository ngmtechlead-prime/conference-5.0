# NGM Conference 5.0 Case Study & Research Analysis Challenge

## About

**Attention all Curious minds!**

Welcome to the NGM Conference 5.0 Case Study and Research Analysis Challenge Competition!

This competition is designed for curious minds ready to explore real-world businesses and economic issues through critical thinking, research, and collaboration. Participants will progress through multiple stages of case analysis, moving from individual insights to team-based challenges, while tackling both Nigerian and Global business scenarios.

If you are passionate about strategy, innovation, and problem-solving, this is your opportunity to showcase your skills and compete with some of the brightest minds. Thank you!
