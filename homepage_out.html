What is NGM Conference?

A single day designed to change trajectories.

NGM Conference 5.0 is the flagship annual gathering of the Nasir Giwa Mentorship Platform — a one-day convergence where students, early-career professionals, founders, and seasoned operators share the same room.

It exists for the people who refuse to wait their turn. The programme is built around keynote sessions, panels, two flagship competitions, and curated networking — all engineered to compress years of access into a single day.
